Frequently asked questions

Does Liquid Calendar have its own cloud sync?
No. Liquid Calendar reads and writes the calendar accounts already available on your iPhone. If your calendar provider syncs across devices, those changes will appear there.
Does the app require an account?
No. The shipped app does not have a Liquid Calendar account system.
How does Pro unlock work?
Liquid Calendar includes a free tier with core calendar features and month view. Pro is a one-time Apple in-app purchase that unlocks the additional views and input features. It does not auto-renew. If you already bought Pro, use Restore Purchases from the paywall while signed in with the same Apple ID.
How do I remove Google Calendar access?
Use the disconnect action inside the app to remove the local Google connection. You can also revoke access from your Google account security settings.
What if reminders are not showing?
Check that notifications are enabled in iOS Settings and that the event itself has an alert configured.
The app is not loading my events. What should I check?
Make sure Liquid Calendar still has calendar permission in iOS Settings and that the calendar you expect is selected inside the app.
